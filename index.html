Approach

How I think before I ship

Same discipline at 7am or midnight — invert what breaks, name the real bottleneck, verify the number moved, then ship what survives contact with reality.

01

Correctness first

Before throughput — what happens on retry, duplicate event, or partial failure? Systems that bill, bind, or reconcile cannot afford silent drift.

02

Measure the bottleneck

Focus beats motivation when the constraint is clear — latency in a transform path, deploy friction slowing teams, a gap in observability. Name it, cut it, confirm it moved.

03

Invert the failure

What would make this fragile at 10× traffic, a bad deploy, or a confused downstream owner — then design so that version does not happen.

04

Own the whole path

From event intake to member outcome — depth over surface area. Clear in review, steady in incident, honest about the next increment.
