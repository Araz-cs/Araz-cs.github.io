Approach

How I think before I ship

Habits from years of owning backends in production — invert the failure modes first, then build toward what survives contact with reality.

01

Correctness first

Before throughput or clever architecture — what happens on retry, duplicate event, or partial failure? Systems that bill, bind, or reconcile cannot afford silent drift.

02

Measure the real bottleneck

Latency hiding in transformation paths, deploy friction slowing teams, observability gaps masking incidents — name it, cut it, verify the number moved.

03

Invert the failure

Ask what would make this fragile at 10× traffic, a bad deploy, or a confused downstream owner — then design so that version of the story does not happen.

04

Own the whole path

From event intake to member outcome — depth over surface area. Clear in review, steady in incident, honest about what the next increment should be.
